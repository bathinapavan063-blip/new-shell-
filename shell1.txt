Key Aspects of a Car
Components: Core parts include the engine (combustion creating power), transmission, wheels, steering, brakes, chassis, and safety features like airbags and seatbelts.
Power Sources: Traditionally petrol or diesel, but electric vehicles (EVs) are rapidly growing, alongside alternatives like CNG.
Types: Vary greatly in design, including sedans (classic four-door), coupes (sportier, two-door), SUVs (larger, off-road capable), hatchbacks, and trucks.
Function: Offers personal freedom, saves time, enables exploration, and provides comfort and convenience for daily travel and emergencies.:wq
